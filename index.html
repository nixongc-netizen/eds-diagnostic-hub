Common questions

Before you start

Can this tell me whether I have hEDS or POTS?

No — and be wary of anything online that claims it can. hEDS is a clinical diagnosis a qualified clinician makes in person, and POTS requires objective heart-rate testing. What this assessment does is show you how closely your symptoms map to the official criteria, so you and your doctor can have a far more productive conversation.

Is it really free?

Yes. The assessment and your full report are free. We also offer optional paid guides for people who want deeper help navigating appointments and care — you'll see them after your report, no obligation.

What happens to my answers?

Your answers stay in your browser — they are never sent to or stored on our servers. Your report is generated on your own device. If you choose to receive your results guide by email, we store only your email address and a general result category, never your individual answers.

Who made this?

Physicians specializing in pain medicine and hypermobility-related conditions, frustrated by how many patients spend years being dismissed before anyone measures their symptoms against criteria that have existed since 2017.

How long does it take?

About 10 minutes. You can move at your own pace — nothing is timed, and every question is one tap.
